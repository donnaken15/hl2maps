Play the Atari classic with fueled multiplayer action!
The object of the game is to rescue the Enchanted Chalice
and place it in your Castle where it belongs. But, beware,
this is no easy task as you will fight an opposing team to
hinder you in your quest for the Enchanted Chalice. Good luck.
